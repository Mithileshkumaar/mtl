About us

At the outset, we take this opportunity to introduce ourselves as Allied Transportation & logistics Pvt Ltd., one of the leading fleet management company in Tamilnadu, since last 11+ years.
Experience and relationships built over 11+ years in the industry, unsurpassed knowledge and a superior service offering, uniquely position ATL to provide innovative Fleet Management to the Corporate & Government markets.
Allied Transportation & Logistics (ATL) Pvt Ltd. offers integrated mobile asset management solutions for corporate and Government customers by providing all types/models of mobile assets (vehicles) on lease or rent and managing efficiently. ATL is known for its comprehensive range of services and value-added facilities derived from proactive relationships with suppliers and manufacturers. Flexibility is combined with demonstrated high levels of customer service and a broad range of products and services. ATL works together with customers to create tailor-made mobility solutions, with a service characterized by openness, flexibility and partnership, which among others include driver motivation and management.
